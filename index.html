The studio

One chair. Her full attention.

Margaret Dolubizno has spent almost three decades caring for hair, and she cares about scalp health just as much. Ask her about it and she's happy to share what she knows.

The salon runs on an organic, 100% ammonia-free color system, and Margaret offers hot-scissor and thermocut techniques for a precise, healthy finish. It's a small, calm space inside Sola Salons in Park Ridge. No rush, no rotating stylists, no guessing.

  • Almost three decades of experience behind the chair
  • Organic, ammonia-free color system
  • A genuine advocate for scalp health, not just the cut
  • A private, one-woman studio, just you and Margaret
